This plugin will extend your parish website.

== Description ==
With this plugin you extend your parish website. With lot’s of cool features.

This plugin will include:

*   Widget with the times of the masses in your parish
*   A administration backend for fill in all necessary data witch are included in the widgets.

== Installation ==
1. From WP admin > Plugins > Add New
1. Search “Kerk en IT Parish” under search and hit Enter
1. Click "Install Now"
1. Click the Activate Plugin link

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= Do I have to fill in duplicate data? =

No, this plugin will minimize the filled data. It’s all connect to each other

= Do you have a roadmap? =

Yes we have. We have great ideas for extending this plugin.
Here some great things in the pipeline. Please provide us feedback for any request.

= I miss data of my language? =
Currently this plugin will work perfectly in the Netherlands. We will add support for your language / country. But we need YOUR help.

= How can I contribute? =
You can help us by providing data of your local dioceses.
Also we are looking for translators. Please contact us, and we will add support for your country.
